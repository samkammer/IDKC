📚 Case Study: Navigating Global Trade with Contract Talent

Client: TerraBloom Imports

Industry: Eco-Friendly Consumer Goods

Challenge: Expanding international sourcing while navigating complex customs and tariff laws

Solution: Contract compliance and logistics experts from The IDKC

Result: 40% faster import cycles, 25% reduction in regulatory costs

🌍 The Challenge

TerraBloom, a small but growing importer of eco-friendly home products, wanted to expand its supply chain to Southeast Asia. But international trade laws, changing tariffs, and customs documentation slowed their progress — and hiring full-time compliance staff wasn’t feasible.

💡 The IDKC Solution

The IDKC placed two experienced contract professionals:

  • A Global Trade Compliance Consultant to manage tariff codes and documentation
  • A Logistics Coordinator to streamline port-to-warehouse processes

Within one week, TerraBloom had the support it needed — without long-term overhead.

📈 The Results

  • 40% faster customs clearance for imports
  • 25% reduction in customs-related fines and fees
  • Zero compliance issues during audits
  • No full-time hires needed
